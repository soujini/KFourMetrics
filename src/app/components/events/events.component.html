Past Events

Pointwise Technology Conference 2018

December 10th – 14th, 2018

Location : Hyderabad, Bangalore, Thiruvananthapuram & Chennai

  • Review of the key happenings at Pointwise, USA including Software features which Pointwise had focused on & key challenges faced.
  • A series of presentations by Pointwise users titled This is how I mesh, focusing on meshing challenges and creating a platform for discussion on features users would like to see in the next version of Pointwise.
  • A summary of upcoming features in Pointwise including applications and discussions on feature-requests from users.

The Future of the Grid in CFD/FEA

November 6th – 10th, 2017

Location : Hyderabad, Pune, Bangalore, Thiruvananthapuram & Chennai

  • Tracing the path taken by engineering simulation over the past 30 years and the challenges ahead.
  • A global overview of CAE in diverse sectors while reflecting on aspects that have had a specific impact on the Indian market such as the abilities to separate mesh generation and simulation.
  • Grid Generation and CAE as related to Simulation time, Cost and Accuracy. Meshing challenges to commercial software developers, current and future needs of CFD users & the short and long-term development concerns and plans for the future.
  • Technology & Development behind Pointwise, the best ways to efficiently produce meshes for accurate simulations, views on the advent of high order flow solvers, FEM using curved meshes and other developments to increase accuracy.
  • Application-case studies related to the CAE design cycle and the roles played by tools like Pointwise in providing unmatched control over the grid as well as Sculptor which allows analysts to modify verified CAE models, either for optimization or design changes.
